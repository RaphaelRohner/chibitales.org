About Chibi Fighters 2.0

What is it?

A game to collect, play and trade virtual cards

  • Buy and own collectable cards
  • Play with your cards
  • Level your cards
  • Skill and equip your Chibi-cards
  • Swap your cards with others
  • Sell your cards on blockchain

Chances

Did you ever spend months in playing and developing a character just to find out you don't own the result?

In Chibi Fighters you

  • own your cards
  • own their progress
  • can trade them as you please

Risks

But - no light without dark

  • Chibi Fighters 2.0 is an Alpha Version
  • There will be bugs
  • It's not free to play
  • and it might not succeed

Card Types

What type of a card are you?

What is it?

Collect and skill different card types

  • Chibis - your equipable fighters
  • Armor
  • Trinkets
  • Skills
  • Weapons

Chibis

Chibis are your main cards you want to level up

You can equip them with

  • Armor - Head + Chest + Legs + Hands + Feet
  • Trinket - One
  • Skill - One
  • Weapon - One

Good to know

Equipping your Chibis has some restrictions:

  • It depends on the Juice available,
  • which depends on your Chibi's level
  • Different slots get available at different levels
  • and it depends on the Chibi and its rarity

Rarity

The Common and the Legendary

What is it?

Rarity describes how many cards of a type exist:

  • Unique ~ 1-4 Chibi cards exist
  • Legendary ~ 1% per edition
  • Mythic ~ 6% per edition
  • Rare ~ 30% per edition
  • Common ~ 63% per edition

Identify

You can easily recognize the rarity of a card:

  • Green frame = unique card
  • Golden frame = legendary card
  • Red frame = mythic card
  • Blue frame = rare card
  • Grey frame = common card

Max Level and Slots

Rarity matters and here is why:

  • Unique - max lvl 200 - 8 slots
  • Legendary - max lvl 200 - 8 slots
  • Mythic - max lvl 150 - 4 slots
  • Rare -max lvl 100 - 2 slots
  • Common - max lvl 50 - no slots

Good to know

Some extra rarity infos:

  • Unique cards don't come from packs
  • So far the rarity of a card can not change
  • Each card type has its unique rarity
  • Meaning: If one of a type is common, all are

Juice

juicy, juicy Juice

What is it?

Yeah, you want this juice !!

  • Each card shows its juice in the top left
  • Juice determines what your Chibi can equip
  • Chibi's Juice minus equipped Juice tells you how much Juice your Chibi has left

How to get Juice

Even on high levels - Juice is always to little !

So, here's how to get it:

  • Each card has its basic Juice of 19
  • Every five levels the card gains one Juice
  • At Level 6 you will have 20
  • At Level 11 you will have 21

Good to know

Keep in mind: Juice of equipable cards is taken from the Chibi's Juice.

  • A high level weapon sounds fantastic, but:
  • If you level it up, it will take more Juice to equip
  • Which means you might not be able to equip another item.
  • And more low level items make your Chibi stronger than one high level item.

Stats

Show me your Stats

What is it?

Stats define your Chibi's strength

  • Juice
  • Level
  • Health
  • Damage
  • Charged Skill

How to raise your Stats

Most of the stats raise by leveling your card up

  • By spending experience to level up your Chibi:
    • Your Level will go up by one / lvl
    • Your Health will go up by one / lvl
    • Your Damage will go up by one / lvl
  • And by equipping other cards

Good to know

Chibis is still in Alpha version, so keep in mind:

  • You can't activate a charged skill yourself
  • Leveling any other card but Chibis isn't useful yet
  • You don't gain much by doing so
  • You lose on rare Juice points by doing so
