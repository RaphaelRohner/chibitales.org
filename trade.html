What is it about?

You own the cards you play. Buy them - sell them!

  • Most games let you play
  • but the content isn't yours
  • In Chibi Fighters 2.0 you own your cards
  • Similar to a physical trading card
  • Swap them! Buy them! Sell them! If you can.

Account ID

Find out your Account ID

  • Go to chibifighters.com
  • Login and go to Account
  • Top of the page you find your Account ID
  • The Account ID is unique.
  • Any card send to this ID is transferred to you

Send Cards

How to send a card

  • In game open collection
  • Click the card type e.g. Hercules
  • Click the Hercules you want to send
  • Select Transfer Card button
  • Enter the ID you want to send to
  • Click the Trolley button
  • Sending a card will cost you ten CHIBS

Attention

There are some things you have to pay attention to:

  • You can make any deal you want
  • But the risk is totally on you if it goes wrong
  • Write down the ID and make sure it's the right one
  • Write down the unique card ID from the bottom right
  • Ask a neutral third from the community to assist
  • Get yourself a wallet and use e.g. Opensea

MetaMask

Welcome to Ethereum

What is it?

New to Ethereum or coming from Tron?

  • Chibis 2.0 isn't Blockchain based anymore
  • You can buy with Paypal and cards are offchain
  • In Chibis 1.0 they were all tokenized
  • But you still can mint cards to the Ethereum chain
  • And for high lvl Raids you need to do so
  • So, you'll need a wallet

Install MetaMask

Seting up a MetaMask Ethereum wallet is easy

  • For example: Desktop, Google Chrome:
  • Go to Settings, Extensions
  • Search for MetaMask and add it.
  • Open MetaMask and select Create a Wallet
  • Set a safe password
  • WRITE DOWN THE PASSWORD PHRASE AND KEEP IT SAFE
  • confirm the phrase and - that's it

Good to know

Some things about wallets you NEED to keep in mind

  • The phrase allows to import it into ANY wallet
  • Anybody getting access to it OWNS your funds
  • NOBODY can help you gain access back if you lose it
  • Keep it safe and keep it physical
  • You'll find a cryptic public address in MM
  • Funds are send from and received by this address
  • You will need to pay some sort of fee for transactions
  • At e.g changelly.com you can buy with Visa

Connect Metamask

Connect your Chibis

What is it?

Chibi Fighters 2.0 cards are not on Chain by default

  • If you want to use Blockchain marketplaces or
  • if you want to play high lvl content in Chibis 2.0
  • you will need to mint to the Chain
  • First you need to connect your Chibi Account with your Ethereum Wallet

Connect to ETH

Connect your Game Account with your Wallet

  • Copy your Public Address from e.g. MetaMask
  • Login into Chibi Fighters
  • Click the Shiney Section in the top right corner
  • It's where your exp is displayed as well
  • Paste your Wallet Key in
  • Confirm by clicking the green tick button

Good to know

Better than Nothing

  • Only connect one Wallet to one Account

Mint

Chain your Chibis

What is it?

Ever wanted to print your own currency?

  • Well not really, but coming close
  • You want to join the lvl50 Raid?
  • Or sell your card on Opensea.io for ETH?
  • Easy: Just mint your Chibis to Ethereum

How to Mint

Minting will make your card accessible from ETH

  • Send some ETH to your wallet
  • Click the card of your choice
  • Click mint, confirm with the ETH button
  • Wait a few minutes and refresh
  • Your new tokenized card will show the Ethereum Logo

Good to know

Some Info on Blockchain Integration

  • Minting will cost you a small fee on ETH chain
  • Chibi 1.0 ETH & TRX tokens don't work in Chibis 2.0
  • Players were given packs and CHIBS for their investment
  • The Unique cards were distributed again
  • So far there is only ETH-Opensea integration
