The Games of Chibi Fighters 2.0

What are the Games?

Chibi Fighters 2.0 includes those games so far

  • Adventures
  • Hunt
  • Raids
  • Match

Goals

Chibis is not just a Collectable Trading Card Game. You can level, develop and equip your cards.

To do so you need to:

  • Collect Experience
  • Earn Raid Points
  • Find loot
  • Compete with other players

Good to know

There are some things you should pay attention to:

  • Don't spend all you earn straight
  • Things are in constant change
  • Take a look at the timely rotations

Adventures

Up for an Adventure?

What is it?

The two Adventures are the Idle component of Chibis:

  • Send out two teams of one to three Chibis to:
  • Courtyard of Death and
  • Pit of Snakes
  • Each equipped Chibi earns experience / minute
  • Start here to progress quickly !!

Earning Exp

To send out a Chibi: Click the Adventures Tab and then a grey slot.

A list of available cards pops up. Just select one to equip.

  • Courtyard of death requires and earns:
  • lvl 1 - 10exp | lvl 5 - 15exp | lvl 10 - 20exp
  • Pit of Snakes requires and earns:
  • lvl 10 - 10exp | lvl 20 - 20exp | lvl 30 - 30exp

Good to know

And here come some tipps for Adventures

  • It's the BEST way to earn exp. Up to 150k in 24h
  • You need to claim the exp earned
  • After eight hours no more exp is added if you don't
  • You just need to equip cards once, but...
  • Check if a Slot got unequipped accidentally

Hunt

Ready for the Hunt?

What is it?

The Hunt is a PvE Mini Game for two to six Chibis

  • Equip two to six Chibis to your party
  • The Hunt requires a ticket to play it
  • You get five tickets at 7pm GMT per day
  • You will earn exp
  • You may find loot
  • Loot can be Chibis, Armor, Weapons, Skills
  • Loot cards are just found ingame

How to Play

Click the Hunt Tab and equip one loadout with 2-6 Chibis

  • Click Start Hunt
  • Your party will face its first opponent
  • After the fight you can claim loot or continue
  • The number of opponents increases every 4 rounds by one up to 6
  • The HP of each opponent raises by 50 each round
  • The DMG of each opponent raises by 5 each round

Good to know

You really want that loot?

  • Drops are random and can be up to two cards at once
  • If you lose a fight, all previous loot is lost!!
  • So pay attention to your teams strength
  • A team of 6 (1/1/5/10/10/20) should stop around stage 12
  • You earn some exp as well, starting at 30 up to 100 from stage 20 on
  • You can stop and restart the Hunt at any point
  • Only claiming your loot or losing will finish a round

Raids

Let's Raid !!

What is it?

Raids are PvE Mini Games for two to six Chibis

  • There are 21 Raids on a seven day rotation
  • Three of those are available each day
  • Raid 1 requires lvl 10 Chibis
  • Raid 2 requires lvl 20 Chibis
  • Raid 3 requires lvl 50 Chibis minted to the Ethereum Chain
  • Each day you get ten Raid tickets at 7pm GMT
  • You can spend them on all three raids

Rewards

Lots of rewards in Raids! Let's take an extra look

  • All Raids will give you min 100exp and 5 Raid Points
  • There is the chance to win 10 to 35 Raid Points
  • Each Raid offers instant loot
  • And one player per raid wins the daily raffle
  • The loot is wide ranged and offers all cards that can drop.
  • Including the Legendary Chibi Wolf once a week in a raffle!
  • Ten players win a nice pack of exp and RP daily per raid

How to Play

Click the Raids Tab and equip one Raid with 2-6 Chibis

  • Click the animation to start a Raid
  • You can fast forward by clicking the green arrow
  • After the fight pick a loot box and open it.
  • You can either continue with the same Raid
  • or chose another one by equiping it
  • Check out the Leader Board under the Raid!

Good to know

Storm the Leader Board !!

  • Raids are quick and easy, don't miss out on them!
  • Under each raid you find its current Leader Board
  • Out of tickets? Just click a raid to buy more for exp
  • Raids drop some of the best cards in game:
  • Chibis - Young Spartan (Rare), Assassin (Mysthic)
  • Weapon - Slicer (Rare)
  • Armor - The Full Rare Cloth Set, currently the best Armor
  • Skill - Waterboarding, attacks up to three opponents

Match

What are you looking at?

What is it?

Your team fights other teams in PvP Matches

  • Matches are not fully done yet, but
  • Any Chibi can be put into your team
  • You can equip one to six Chibis
  • You have an attacking and a defending team
  • You can use the same Chibis for both

How to Play Matches

Click the Match Tab and equip your team

  • So far you can play Match without limits
  • But a limit of 10 fights a day is in planning
  • After setting up your two teams click Search
  • The Game will search for worthy opponents
  • Scroll through them and pick whichever you like
  • Click the attack button and enjoy the Match

Good to know

So far you just see how well your team competes

  • A win will earn you 100 points
  • A lose will take 30 points of your ballance
  • You see your rank at the top of the page
  • After clicking Search you might need to scroll down.
  • There should be four to five opponent teams
  • You can attack empty teams and still earn points
