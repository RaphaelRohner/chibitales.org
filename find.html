From players for players

Hi there and welcome to chibitales.org!

This page is the result of being obsessed with Chibi Fighters since it came to the Tron blockchain. We are not associated in any other way with the owners of Chibi Fighters, except that we got the OK to make this fanpage.

We try to keep the page as up to date as possible, but it's a one person project and completly voluntary. So, sorry in advance if some information is missing or outdated.

If you want to give feedback or input, please contact Rorschach on either Discord or Telegram for now.

One last word. Putting money into Crypto is a high risk, just spend what you are willing to lose.
